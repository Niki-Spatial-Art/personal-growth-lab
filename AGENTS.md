# Personal Growth Lab Instructions

## Scope

This repository is for public, privacy-safe personal growth frameworks.

Use it for:

- habit systems
- fitness and wellness frameworks
- beauty/style planning frameworks
- lightweight self-review templates
- public resources and playbooks

Do not publish private health logs, exact medical details, private relationship details, private schedules, photos, identity-sensitive details, or raw personal chat transcripts.

## Preferred Codex Routing

- Use `personal-beauty-board` for beauty, nutrition, fitness, skincare, body composition, and style planning.
- Use `Documents` for structured plans.
- Use `Spreadsheets` when tracking habits, budgets, or progress tables.
- Use `Notion` when connected notes need to be captured or summarized.

## Output Style

- Keep public content framework-based.
- Avoid medical claims.
- Use gentle, practical plans rather than extreme routines.
- Separate private personal logs from public reusable templates.

## Safety

If a task involves medical, legal, or high-stakes personal advice, verify current sources when needed and keep recommendations conservative.
